Building My Future in IT, One Step at a Time

Where My Interest in IT Started

      Technology has always been part of my daily life. From waking up and checking my phone to using a computer for school work, I am constantly surrounded by technology. At first, I only used it for entertainment and communication, but over time, I became curious about what happens behind the screen.

      I realized that technology is not just about devices, but also about solving problems and making life easier for people. This realization sparked my interest in the IT field. I believe that learning technology will open many opportunities for my future.

My Career Goal and Why I Want It

      In the next five years, I see myself working as an IT professional. I want a career where I can use my skills to solve real-world problems. I enjoy analyzing issues and finding solutions, which is why IT feels like the right path for me.

      The IT field continues to grow as technology evolves. This career offers stability, growth, and continuous learning— things that motivate me to pursue it.

IT Careers I Am Interested In

      There are several IT careers that caught my interest because they align with my skills and personality:

  • Software Developer – I enjoy creating things and turning ideas into functional programs.
  • Web Developer – I want to build clean, useful, and interactive web pages.
  • IT Support Specialist – I enjoy helping people and explaining technical problems clearly.

Skills I Need to Improve

      Having a goal is not enough; I also need the right skills. The IT industry is competitive, so preparation is important.

  1. Basic programming skills – helps develop logical thinking and problem-solving.
  2. Communication skills – important for teamwork and clarity.
  3. Time management – helps balance school, responsibilities, and life.

Looking Ahead to My IT Future

      Step by step, I am building my future in IT. With patience, dedication, and hard work, I believe I can achieve the career I dream of.

Lusanta, John Eugene R. - ITAD
